### Week 3 Objectives

* Master Amazon RDS (Relational Database Service) fundamentals and supported engines (MySQL, PostgreSQL, Aurora).
* Learn High Availability design with Multi-AZ Deployment and read scaling with Read Replicas.
* Explore NoSQL databases with Amazon DynamoDB (Tables, Primary Key, Sort Key, Indexing) and In-Memory Caching with Amazon ElastiCache.

### Week 3 Achievements

* Mastered provisioning, securing, and connecting Amazon RDS instances inside Private Subnets.
* Understood automated backup mechanisms and Multi-AZ High Availability failover capabilities.
* Mastered NoSQL data modeling, secondary indexing (GSI/LSI), and data querying on Amazon DynamoDB.
* Evaluated and implemented In-Memory Caching to accelerate database response times.
