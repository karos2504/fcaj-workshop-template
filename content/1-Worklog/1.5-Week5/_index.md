### Week 5 Objectives

* Understand AWS Monitoring, Logging, and Observability principles.
* Gain proficiency in Amazon CloudWatch (Metrics, Dashboards, Alarms & Logs Insights).
* Integrate CloudWatch Alarms with Amazon SNS notifications and explore CloudWatch & Grafana integration.
