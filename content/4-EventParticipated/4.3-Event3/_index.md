# Summary Report: FCAJ Community Day

## Event Objectives

- Understand the transition and strategic journey of FCAJ becoming an AWS Partner.
- Learn the career path, essential skills, and steps to become a Data Analyst.
- Explore the organizational culture and communication patterns in a professional business environment.
- Discover the roadmap, tools, and mindset required to become a DevOps Engineer.

## Key Highlights

- Strategic vision shared by industry leaders on the partnership between FCAJ and AWS.
- Comprehensive guide to mastering data tools, analytics, and business intelligence for aspiring Data Analysts.
- Practical workshop on workplace culture, collaboration, and professional ethics.
- Real-world DevOps transition stories and core infrastructure automation practices.

---

## Session 1: FCAJ to AWS partner

### Topics Covered

- The vision and roadmap for FCAJ to transition into an AWS Partner.
- Benefits of the partnership for the community, students, and businesses.
- Requirements, certifications, and competencies needed to achieve partner status.
- Collaboration opportunities and upcoming initiatives under the AWS Partner Network (APN).

### Key Takeaways

- The AWS partnership enhances FCAJ's credibility and access to advanced cloud resources.
- Community members gain access to specialized training and joint programs.
- Developing AWS certified talents is crucial for achieving and maintaining partnership status.

---

## Session 2: How to become Data Analysis

### Topics Covered

- The role of a Data Analyst in modern data-driven enterprises.
- Core technical skills: SQL, Python, Excel, and BI tools (PowerBI, Tableau, QuickSight).
- Critical soft skills: business domain understanding and data storytelling.
- Building a portfolio with real-world datasets and showcase projects.

### Key Takeaways

- Data analytics is not just about technical tools, but about generating actionable business insights.
- Strong fundamentals in SQL and data modeling are essential.
- Communication and storytelling are key to bridging the gap between data and business decisions.

---

## Session 3: Culture in business

### Topics Covered

- Defining organizational culture and its impact on personal growth.
- Effective communication patterns in professional environments.
- Collaboration dynamics, accountability, and professional ethics.
- Navigating conflicts and feedback loops in corporate settings.

### Key Takeaways

- Company culture is a critical driver for team alignment and productivity.
- Open, honest, and prompt communication is the key to successful collaboration.
- Receiving constructive feedback gracefully is essential for career development.

---

## Session 4: How to become DevOps

### Topics Covered

- The DevOps philosophy: breaking silos between development and operations.
- Core skills: CI/CD pipelines, Infrastructure as Code (IaC), monitoring, and cloud services.
- Transitioning from software engineering or system administration to DevOps.
- Key automation tools: GitHub Actions, Terraform, AWS CloudFormation, and Docker.

### Key Takeaways

- DevOps is a cultural mindset of automation, continuous improvement, and shared responsibility.
- Mastery of version control, CI/CD, and IaC is mandatory.
- Automating repetitive tasks reduces errors and speeds up software delivery cycles.

---

## Event Experience & Learnings

The **FCAJ Community Day** provided rich insights into corporate alignment, career preparation, and specialized technical fields like Data Analysis and DevOps.

### Key Learnings

- **Partnership Growth**: The FCAJ to AWS Partner roadmap shows the strategic direction of the community.
- **Career Pathways**: Gained clear, actionable roadmaps for pursuing specialized tracks like Data Analysis and DevOps.
- **Cultural Fit**: Learned the importance of adapting to and building positive organizational cultures.
